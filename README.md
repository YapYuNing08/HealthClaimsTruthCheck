# 🛡️ BetulKe Health 

> **A zero-friction digital safety net protecting Malaysians from dangerous medical misinformation and predatory health scams.**

🔗 **[Click here to view the BetulKe Health App]**(https://partyrock.aws/u/Ning08/dIYXJ7VgH/Health-Claims-Truth-Check)

## ⚠️ The Problem
In Malaysia, health misinformation is a critical public safety crisis. Encrypted group chats are often flooded with unverified "miracle cures," while scammers use AI deepfakes to sell unregistered, dangerous supplements to the elderly. Vulnerable users often lack the digital literacy to verify these claims, and navigating complex government reporting portals is intimidating, resulting in massive underreporting.

## 💡 The Solution
**BetulKe Health** is an elder-centric, safety-first HealthTech application built to stop the "WhatsApp Misinformation Epidemic." By replacing complex chatbots with a seamless, single-screen analysis, it empowers everyday citizens to safely fact-check medical claims and instantly report scams to authorities.

### 🚀 Core Features
* **🇲🇾 Hyper-Localized Intelligence:** Hardcoded to understand the Malaysian healthcare context, Ministry of Health (KKM/NPRA) banned product guidelines, and regional health myths.
* **🌐 Native Multilingual Support:** Fully supports English, Bahasa Melayu, Mandarin, and Tamil to ensure accessibility for all communities.
* **👵 Zero-Friction UX:** No "prompt engineering" required. Users simply upload a screenshot or paste a message to get an instant analysis.
* **📋 Step-by-Step Triage:** Replaces dense medical jargon with clear, actionable crisis management: 🛑 **Stop**, 🩺 **Monitor**, 📞 **Call/Visit Clinic**.
* **🚨 1-Click Government Reporting:** Automatically drafts professional complaints and routes users to the MCMC Aduan portal or NSRC 997 hotline, reducing reporting time from **45 minutes to just 20 seconds**.

## ⚙️ Architecture & Technology
Built entirely on **AWS PartyRock**, BetulKe Health utilizes advanced **Prompt Chaining** to execute parallel processing.
